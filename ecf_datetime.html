Equivalent to from_now/2 but with a single argument, `#(,n ,unit).

from_now/1 will also accept a non-negative integer, n,
and treat it as `#(,n seconds).

Example Usage

> (from_now 42)
1458994427
> (from_now #(0 days))
1458994387
> (from_now #(1 hour))
1458997992
> (from_now #(60 minutes))
1458997996
> (from_now (+ (days 1) (hours 6)))
1459102403
